Auction Mobility Migration

After 2 years improving Lofty.com systems, Auction Mobility bought the start-up and we rebuilt 60% of Lofty's code at front-ends to deal with auctions instead of market places.

Main challenges:
  • Migrate from a Shopify Plus, with full support and complete API resources like triggers, hooks, and no-limit access to a basic account.
  • Change the business core from a market place solution to just a show case of auction's items.
  • To support multi currencies for auctions houses in the whole world.
  • Migrate all heavy images from Amazon S3 cdn to Auction Mobility's owns servers.
  • Build up new product's collections based on Auction Houses.
  • Categorize items based on theirs descriptions texts, once the appraisal process was discontinued.
