About

It's me!

A codesigner, also called full stack developer, who works at UOL since 2011.

I'm coding since 2002 in several different languages, and I'm still in love with web development, dazzled with the opportunities that internet brought to me, like the continuous learning environment and the infinite challenge source.

For me, the world wide web is a true provider of real chances to improve the society, connecting people, business and governments.

By the way, I'm always trying to find out some time to play with my daughter, see my friends and have some fun, and find a joke to make everybody laugh a lot, and out loud.
