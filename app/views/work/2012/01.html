UOL: form filling extension

While working at UOL's platform, to test our systems was really pain full and we used to create new users all the time, full filling a signup form, repeatedly.

There was also an out of date full fill form extension to help out generating new users, but within the years it didn't follow all the rules updates.

Results:

  • The extension became able to generate user again and came back to life.
  • Had a algorithm to only generates odd CPF (IDs) numbers, which were accepted without validation at the staging environment.
  • Works with different Xpaths covering sign-up forms of different products.
  • Generates valid e-mails on @mailinator, prefixed with the creation date and the product in use to easy debugging.
  • Creates valid but fake Credit Cards using LUHN algorithm
  • Generates random and funny names and last names, including famous UOL colleagues nick names easily customizable locally
