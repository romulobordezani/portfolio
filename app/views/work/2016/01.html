Lofty's e-Shop migration from Magento to Shopify

The first version of Lofty's site was made using Magento, my main role on this job was to migrate the whole e-shop to Shopify, what required a reconstruction of almost eery HTML and CSS from the scratch, using .liquid and based on Shopify's Timber boilerplate.

Main challenges:

  • To migrate all CDN data from Magento to Shopify, including +10Mb pictures, what required a hard work converting and compressing images in order to upload them using Ruby, via Shopify API.
  • To convert all products and users stored in the Magento's MySQL database, into Shopify records using theirs Ruby API.
  • To fight with Form Assembly pre built forms to improve validations, styles and so on conflicting with Timber style.
  • To reproduce all old design details on a boilerplate already formatted and coded, inheriting a lot of conflicts and incompatibilities between them.
  • To deal with third party apps and partners, implementing theirs snippets on our site without to wreck all the rest of the code.
  • To keep the loading time under 10 seconds, even using at least 7 third party services and make the site resilient to possible slow responses or down services.
  • To track important user's events on different platforms, like Facebook Pixel, Sales Force Marketing Cloud and Google Analytics at the same time.
  • To fight with Form Assembly pre built forms to improve validations, styles and so on.
  • To make it responsive.
